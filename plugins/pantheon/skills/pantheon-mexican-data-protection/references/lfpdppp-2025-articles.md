# LFPDPPP 2025 Article-by-Article Reference

Effective March 21, 2025. Published in DOF March 20, 2025. Completely replaces 2010 law.

## Key articles for audit

| Article | Topic | Audit checkpoint |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Object and scope | Verify processor in scope of law (Mexican-resident data subjects) |
| 6 §2 | AI/automated-decision objection right | Verify on-demand explanation and opt-out mechanism |
| 7 | Sensitive data | Identify all sensitive-category data, verify explicit consent or legal basis |
| 8 | Consent | Verify granularity, withdrawal mechanism, age-of-consent |
| 23 | Right to object (ARCO+) | Verify mechanism exists separate from access right |
| 36 | Processor obligations | Verify processor agreements where applicable |
| 44 | Personal liability of processor | Verify processor (not just controller) signs accountability |
| 50 | Processor agreements | Verify Articles 50-58 contract template used |
| 71 | Cross-border transfer | Verify equivalent-obligations clause in any non-Mexican processor contract |
| 80+ | Sanctions | 100 to 320,000 UMAs = ~MXN $3.5M to $17.6M+ |
| 102 | Specialized federal data-protection courts | Verify legal-counsel awareness of jurisdiction |
