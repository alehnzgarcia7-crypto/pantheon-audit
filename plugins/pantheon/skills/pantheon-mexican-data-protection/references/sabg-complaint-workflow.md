# SABG Complaint Workflow

Replaces the dissolved INAI. The Secretariat of Anti-Corruption and Good Governance (SABG) is an executive-branch body, raising independence concerns the auditor must flag in deliverables.

## Workflow
1. Data subject files complaint with SABG (not INAI; INAI dissolved March 21, 2025)
2. SABG initial review within 30 calendar days
3. Resolution within 6 to 12 months typical
4. Sanctions: temporary or permanent processing suspension, fines 100 to 320,000 UMAs
5. Appeal to specialized federal data-protection courts (creation within 120 days of LFPDPPP 2025 effective date)

## Audit checkpoints
- Verify all data-subject-facing UIs reference SABG, not INAI
- Verify complaint endpoint includes SABG mailing address and electronic intake
- Verify legal counsel is registered with current SABG procedures
- Flag any reference to INAI in policies, privacy notices, contracts

## Caveat for audit narrative
SABG independence is contested by civil-society groups. PANTHEON audit narrative should note this without taking political position; recommend that critical fintech operations maintain independent legal-counsel relationships beyond SABG procedural reliance.
