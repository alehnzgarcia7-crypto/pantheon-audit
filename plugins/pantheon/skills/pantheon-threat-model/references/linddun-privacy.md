# LINDDUN Privacy Threat Modeling

KU Leuven. Privacy STRIDE-equivalent.

## Categories
- Linkability
- Identifiability
- Non-repudiation (privacy negative)
- Detectability
- Disclosure
- Unawareness
- Non-compliance

## When to apply
PII, PHI, behavioral data, GDPR/LFPDPPP/HIPAA/CCPA/PIPEDA scope.

## Mitigations
- Linkability: pseudonymization, unlinkable credentials
- Identifiability: k-anonymity, differential privacy
- Non-repudiation: ring signatures, deniable encryption
- Detectability: dummy traffic, ORAM, mixnets
- Disclosure: encryption at rest/transit, access control
- Unawareness: transparent notices, consent
- Non-compliance: privacy by design, DPIA

Reference: Deng et al. (2011). linddun.org
